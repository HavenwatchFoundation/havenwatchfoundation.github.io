Who we are

Havenwatch is a civilian-founded, non-law-enforcement organization that protects children online by identifying risks, compiling lawful evidence, and supporting safe reporting. We operate with strict legal boundaries, trauma-informed handling, and layered operational security to make sure work is both effective and defensible. Our volunteers range from analysts and legal reviewers to content creators and operations staff — all trained to follow clear SOPs and escalation paths.

What we do

We collect referrals and tipline reports, verify whether incoming evidence meets our internal guidelines, and prepare packages for lawful handover to platforms, NGOs, or law enforcement where appropriate. We also produce public-facing educational content that helps prevent online harm without sensationalizing cases.

Intake & Triage
Front-line intake: verify evidence, summarize reports, approve or reject cases, and request more info from reporters or partner forwarders (securely and under supervision). Roles: Intake Reviewer, Intake Lead.
Digital Investigations & Analysis
OSINT-only analysis to map digital footprints and context. No evidence validation or HUMINT unless explicitly authorized. Roles: Digital Investigator, Analysis Specialist.
Legal, Policy & External Relations
Legal screening, policy design, and packaging evidence for lawful submission. Roles: Field Legal Reviewer, Policy Advisor, Cybertipping & External Relations Specialist.
Operations & Case Management
Assigns and prioritizes cases, manages access, enforces inactivity policies, and oversees continuity. Roles: Operations Coordinator, Case Management Officer, Internal Compliance Officer.
Communications, Education & Outreach
Creates evidence-based public education and awareness materials. Roles: Content Creator, Editor & Publisher, Researcher.

Volunteer engagement & activity

Most operational roles require consistent activity. Typical expectations:

  • Minimum activity: 2–3 reports processed or 1–2 hours per week for most roles.
  • Security policy: accounts inactive for 2 weeks will be temporarily removed or restricted for operational security (OPSEC).
  • Reinstatement: volunteers may request reactivation by checking in and completing a supervised task assigned by Operations or Internal Compliance.
Important: Some senior roles or legal advisory positions may not require weekly activity and can operate in advisory or reserve capacity. Senior roles are selectively assigned based on performance and trust.

Safety, privacy & legal boundaries

Havenwatch does not act as law enforcement. We operate within national and international legal frameworks, maintain strict confidentiality, and limit access to sensitive material. All public content is reviewed by Legal to avoid sensationalism or exposure culture. Volunteers are trained to follow evidence-handling SOPs and report any concerns through internal compliance.
