Havenwatch

Who we are

Havenwatch is a civilian-founded initiative supporting lawful reporting, evidence organization, and online safety education.

Status note (Jan 2026): We are in the process of officially registering as an NGO. We currently operate as a volunteer initiative.

What we do

We review referrals, assess whether material meets reporting standards, and prepare reports for lawful submission to platforms, NGOs, or authorities.

Departments

Intake & Triage

Screens reports, checks completeness, and routes cases securely.

Digital Analysis

OSINT-based context review and pattern analysis.

Legal & Policy

Ensures compliance and prepares lawful reporting packages.

Operations

Case assignment, access control, and workflow oversight.

Education & Outreach

Public awareness and prevention-focused content.

Safety & Legal Boundaries

We are not law enforcement. We work within legal frameworks and confidentiality standards.
