Who we are

Havenwatch is a civilian-founded, non-law-enforcement initiative working to reduce online risks to children through lawful reporting support, evidence organization, and public education.

Status note (Jan 2026): Havenwatch is currently in the process of officially registering as an NGO. At present, we operate as a civilian volunteer initiative.

What we do

We review referrals, assess whether material meets reporting guidelines, and prepare structured reports for lawful handover to platforms, NGOs, or authorities. We also create prevention-focused educational content.

Safety & legal boundaries

We do not act as law enforcement. We operate within legal frameworks, maintain confidentiality, and follow strict SOPs for handling sensitive information.

Reporting & Support

If you encounter suspected child exploitation or endangerment online, reporting directly to authorities or trusted hotlines is often the fastest way to protect a child.

NCMEC CyberTipline (US & international reports):
report.cybertip.org

You can also report to your local law enforcement or national child protection organizations. Early reporting helps professionals intervene safely.
