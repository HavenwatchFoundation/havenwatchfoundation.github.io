Who we are

Havenwatch is a civilian-founded initiative supporting lawful reporting, evidence organization, and online safety education.

Status note (Jan 2026): Havenwatch is in the process of officially registering as an NGO. We currently operate as a volunteer initiative.

What we do

We review referrals, assess whether material meets reporting standards, and prepare reports for lawful submission to platforms, NGOs, or authorities. We emphasize trauma-aware, evidence-based handling and strict legal compliance.
